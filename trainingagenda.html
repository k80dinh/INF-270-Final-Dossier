Training Agenda

Senior level positions for Digital and Technology Management require a diverse blend of hard and soft skills that can be utilized across many different software systems used in information technology rather than one specific programming language. This can make pathways into Information Science less straightforward than some STEM careers.

Heather herself began as a print journalism and political science major. Rather than targeting a particular degree or set of classes, gaining a broader understanding of how different digital systems fit into a single organizational plan is ideal for this field.
