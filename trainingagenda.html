Training Agenda

6) Training agenda: In order to enter this profession, what kinds of preparation should someone at your professional level undertake? Are there particular classes they should take? Should they be looking for particular kinds of internships? Should they target particular technical skills?

Senior level positions for Digital and Technology Management require a diverse blend of hard and soft skills that can be utilized across various different software systems used in information technology rather than focusing on one programming language. This can make pathways into Information Science less straightforward than some STEM careers. Heather herself, for example, was a print journalism and political science major. Rather than targeting a particular degree or set of classes, gaining a broader understanding of how digital systems fit together within an organizational plan is ideal preparation for this field.
