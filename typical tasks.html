Typical Tasks

General Tasks

  • Negotiate with vendors
  • Data Management
  • Operating a DAMS or CMS
  • UI/UX Research and Design
  • Collections digitization
  • Collaboration across departments

Especially for jobs of lower seniority, operating a DAMS or CMS, data management and digitization are recurrent tasks. Additionally, systems within GLAM sites are not limited to the backend. UI/UX design and creating digital interactives for public display are also common job responsibilities—particularly if an institution has a strong exhibition culture.

Heather's Role

As one moves up the seniority ladder, tasks involve managerial decision-making and supervision. Hart does not actually participate in the physical creation of a working system or the digitization of the collection. Rather, she identifies specific goals and desired features for a system through staff input and hires professionals with the necessary technical skills to carry out these objectives within the allocated budget. Her daily responsibilities are as followed:

  • Collaboration with all departments to ensure that all information systems meet the needs of each division. User-experience surveys are frequently dispersed to identify possible improvements.
  • Negotiation with vendors when outsourcing software or labor to ensure product fits within budget and the needs of the institution.
  • Problem-solving consultations to devise solutions for information needs using existing systems within the digital infrastructure.
