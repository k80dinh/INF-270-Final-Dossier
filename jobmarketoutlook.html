Job-Market Outlook

However, as technology advances, software continues to be developed and refined, and more and more collections are digitized, this field is growing. Likewise, there are many jobs that can fall under this umbrella, and they are not exclusive to MLIS degree holders. A range of applicable skills, including IT work, can lead to a technology-based role, particularly when it comes to entry-level positions. As Heather shared, these hard skills are particularly important for securing entry-level work in a GLAM technology space. These roles rely on technical capabilities and software training, as well as adaptability when it comes to working with a range of softwares. To advance towards a position like Heather’s, there is no straightforward path. Instead, various entry points exist, like IT roles, technical assistant positions, and more. As one then progresses in their career, they can gain skills in leadership, organization, management, creativity, problem solving, and cultural heritage preservation that may support them in a technological leadership role in a GLAM institution. A leader in this position should be able to understand the internal infrastructure of a GLAM institution, as well as how to manage technology and organize various departments to achieve the institution’s goals. Likewise, it is important to remember not all digital and technological roles associated with GLAM institutions are internal. Often, an organization will hire outside vendors to work on specialized projects, like developing digital, interactive material for a museum exhibit. Budget constraints may also impact institutions, like Heather mentioned, and limit their ability to hire their own technology or design specialists. Furthermore, as mentioned earlier, roles and necessary skills will also vary greatly based on an institution’s goals, specialization, and resources. Heather also acknowledges that AI may impact the type of roles available for individuals, and advises one be adaptable when considering job positions and career growth.
