Training Agenda

6) Training agenda: In order to enter this profession, what kinds of preparation should someone at your professional level undertake? Are there particular classes they should take? Should they be looking for particular kinds of internships? Should they target particular technical skills?

Senior level positions for Digital and Technology Management requires a diverse blend of hard and soft skills that can be utilized across various different softwares and programs utilized in information technology rather than one specific programming language to know. This can make “pathways” into IS less straightforward than a STEM career, which is demonstrated by Heather herself, who was a print journalism and political science major.

Rather than targeting a particular degree or set of classes, gaining a broader understanding of the kind of the thought process that goes into fitting different digital systems into a single organizational plan are ideal for this field.
